ABOUT:

Half-Life 2 is a 2004 first-person shooter video game created by Valve that released in 2004. Development for this game lasted 5 years and costed $40 million. The game features combat, exploration, jumping challenges, and puzzle-solving, and narrative elements through scripted sequences.

PLATFORMS:

Half-Life 2 was released on Steam on November 16, 2004. It won 39 game of the year awards and has been cited as one of the most influential FPS games and among the best games ever made. Eventually, the game was ported to the Xbox, Xbox 360, Playstation3, Linux, and MacOS. By 2011, half a million copies were sold. In 2006 and 2007, Half Life released Episodes 1 and Episodes 2 as sequels for Half-Life, but unfortantley in 2020, Half-Life 3 was cancelled, and valve released a prequel called Half-Life: Alyx.
